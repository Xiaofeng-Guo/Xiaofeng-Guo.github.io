Xiaofeng Guo

I am a PhD student in Robotics Institute at Carnegie Mellon University (CMU), advised by Guanya Shi and Sebastian Scherer . I also collaborated with Wenzhen Yuan . I received my bachelor's degree in Mechanical Engineering at Tsinghua university in 2020.

My research focuses on grasping and manipulation, tactile sensing, reinforcement learning and control. Feel free to reach out for collaboration and discussion of research ideas.

Email: xguo2@andrew.cmu.edu / xiaofengguo_cmu@163.com
